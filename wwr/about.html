History

Whiterriver is located at Johannesburg South farm called Mini-treats. It is owned by the Ratema's family. The family enjoys sharing their property with everyone who is looking for an adverture or peace and quite. They have a venue they offer for weddings,kidsparty and babyshers. So, whatever the occassion come and a Mini-treat.
